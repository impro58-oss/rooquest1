### Partnership Evaluation Framework

**Scoring System: 1-5 scale across 6 criteria (max 30 points)**

| Criteria | Weight | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| **Strategic Alignment** | 25% | Mission/vision compatibility, long-term fit |
| **Market Access** | 20% | Customer base overlap, new segment penetration |
| **Capability Gap Fill** | 20% | Technical, financial, or operational resources provided |
| **Revenue Potential** | 20% | Direct revenue + indirect synergy value |
| **Implementation Complexity** | 10% | Time to value, resource requirements |
| **Risk Profile** | 5% | Financial stability, reputation, dependency risk |

**Scoring Rubric:**
- 5 = Exceptional fit, immediate value, low risk
- 4 = Strong fit, clear value proposition
- 3 = Moderate fit, requires work to realize value
- 2 = Weak fit, significant concerns
- 1 = Poor fit, avoid
